# Melexis — 10-Year History, Trajectory & Analyst Sentiment

> A high-quality, family-controlled auto-sensor compounder whose stock is a clean read on the **automotive semiconductor cycle** + structural **content-per-car** growth. Companions: [factual](../03_companies/MELEXIS.md), [narrative](../05_narratives/MELEXIS_narrative.md). Research date 2026-06-28.

## 1. The financial arc (sales)

## 2. Dividend history (the income story)
Melexis pays out most of its earnings: total DPS **€1.90 (2015) → €2.00 (2016) → €2.10 (2017) → €2.20 (2018–2019) → … → €3.70 (2025)** ([Melexis](https://www.melexis.com/en/investors/share-information/dividend)). A capital-light, fabless, high-payout model — owned partly *for* the yield.

## 3. Perception evolution
1. **2015–2018 — "the quiet content-growth compounder":** out-growing the auto-semi market 2×, a hidden Belgian quality name.
2. **2019 — "the cyclical reminder":** −14% sales; the stock de-rated — a textbook auto-semi down-cycle (a useful *pre-COVID* comparable for how Melexis trades a downturn).
3. **2020–2022 — "the shortage super-cycle":** COVID chip shortage + EV content → record sales, re-rate.
4. **2023–2025 — "the inventory correction":** the post-shortage destock; sales rolled over to the 2025 trough.
5. **2026 — "the China-localization + recovery bet":** the "China Speed" WFOE/fab pivot + the guided H2-2026 recovery (see [narrative map](../05_narratives/MELEXIS_narrative.md)).

## 4. Analyst sentiment (Jun 2026)
- Quality auto-semi with a premium for margins + dividend; the debate is **timing the cyclical inflection** (trough H1-26 → recovery H2-26) vs the structural China-localization growth. See current valuation (~€81.50) and guidance (H1 GM ~40%, OM ~17%) in the [factual file](../03_companies/MELEXIS.md).
- **The key analyst frame:** Melexis is a *cyclical-recovery + content-growth* call, NOT an AI stock — read it off auto production (SAAR), the inventory cycle, and peer prints (NXP/Infineon/STMicro), never AI-capex.
