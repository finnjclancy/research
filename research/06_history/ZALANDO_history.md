# Zalando — 10-Year History, Trajectory & Analyst Sentiment

> The textbook **COVID-darling round-trip**: IPO growth story → €105 pandemic peak → post-pandemic bust → profitability pivot → scaled-platform recovery. The stock is *still* far below its 2021 high. Companions: [factual](../03_companies/ZALANDO.md), [narrative](../05_narratives/ZALANDO_narrative.md). Research date 2026-06-28.

---

## 1. The arc
**IPO & early growth (2014–2019):** Zalando IPO'd on Frankfurt **1 Oct 2014** — Germany's largest tech IPO since 2000 — at ~€2.2bn revenue. It compounded the top line at **~21%/yr** post-IPO, prioritising growth/scale over profit ([Zalando equity story](https://corporate.zalando.com/en/investor-relations/zalandos-equity-story-why-invest)).

**★ The COVID boom (2020–2021):** the pandemic was transformational — **2020 revenue +~47%**; market cap hit an **all-time high ~$28.4bn**; the stock peaked at **€105.90 in July 2021**; Zalando joined the **DAX 40** ([Statista](https://www.statista.com/topics/7153/zalando/)).

**★ The post-pandemic bust (2022–2023):** as stores reopened, the online wave reversed — **2022 revenue €10.34bn (flat)**, profit slump, rising logistics costs, weak consumer confidence. The stock **fell >40% from the July-2021 top**; market cap collapsed to **~$6.2bn (2023)**. Management **pivoted hard to profitability** ([Retail Gazette](https://www.retailgazette.co.uk/blog/2023/03/zalando-revenues-slipping/), [WWD](https://wwd.com/business-news/financial/zalandos-q1-results-suggest-pandemic-related-boom-is-over-1235173389/)).

**The recovery (2024–2026):** profitability restored + growth resuming; **ABOUT YOU acquired/integrated**, AI-driven discovery, B2B (ZEOS) scaling — FY2025 GMV €17.6bn, adj EBIT €591m. But at **~€26.68**, the stock is still **~75% below its 2021 peak** (see [narrative map](../05_narratives/ZALANDO_narrative.md)).

## 2. Perception evolution
1. **2014–2019 — "the European online-fashion growth IPO":** scale-over-profit.
2. **2020–2021 — "the COVID e-commerce darling":** €105, DAX 40, priced for permanent online shift.
3. **2022–2023 — "the post-pandemic bust":** the online wave reversed; profitability questioned.
4. **2024–2026 — "the scaled platform + AI recovery":** profitable growth, ecosystem (B2C+B2B), share-runway story — but fighting Shein/Temu.

## 3. The lesson
Zalando is a clean case of **how a demand-pull-forward narrative round-trips**: the market extrapolated COVID online penetration as permanent, then de-rated brutally on normalisation. A useful template for pricing any "structural shift" that may be a pull-forward.

## 4. Analyst sentiment (Jun 2026)
- Moderate Buy, PT ~€31 vs ~€26.7 spot — upside *if* growth + margin both deliver vs Shein/Temu; the bear is thin margins + EU-consumer cyclicality — see [factual file §0/§8](../03_companies/ZALANDO.md).
- **The debate:** scaled-platform compounder (3.5%→15% share) vs margin-compressed by the Chinese ultra-fast-fashion invasion. **Watch GMV + margin together.**
