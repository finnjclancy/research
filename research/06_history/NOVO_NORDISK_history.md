# Novo Nordisk — 10-Year History, Trajectory & Analyst Sentiment

> A rare stock that did the **full boom-and-bust within one decade**: from the **2016 insulin-pricing crash**, to the **GLP-1 supernova** that made it Europe's most valuable company (2021–2024), to the **−75% CagriSema/Lilly bust** (2024–2026). Companions: [factual](../03_companies/NOVO_NORDISK.md), [narrative](../05_narratives/NOVO_NORDISK_narrative.md). Research date 2026-06-28.

---

## 1. The three acts
**★ Act 1 — the insulin-pricing crash (2015–2016):** Novo's core was diabetes/insulin. US **insulin pricing scrutiny** (Novo/Sanofi "shadow pricing"; ~$450/month by 2016; a **2017 Minnesota AG investigation**) + pricing pressure + competition crushed the stock — Novo **fell ~40% in 2015–16**, CEO Lars Rebien Sørensen departed (2016), Lars Fruergaard Jørgensen took over (2017) ([CSRxP](https://www.csrxp.org/fact-sheet-big-pharma-brings-insulin-price-gouging-playbook-to-blockbuster-weight-loss-drugs/)). *A clean pre-COVID example of Novo trading on US-pricing + competition fear.*

**★ Act 2 — the GLP-1 supernova (2017–2024):** semaglutide changed everything. **Ozempic** (diabetes) then **Wegovy** (obesity, FDA-approved & US-launched **June 2021**). Demand exploded — by 2022, **~two-thirds of Denmark's entire GDP growth** was attributed to pharma; Wegovy/Ozempic were **~55% of 2023 revenue**; profits +45% in H1-23; revenue **+26% in 2024** (~$14.6bn net profit). Novo became **Europe's most valuable company**, peaking **>DKK 1,000 in mid-2024** ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2025/03/31/ozempic-wegovy-novo-nordisk-nvidia/)).

**★ Act 3 — the bust (2024–2026):** the pipeline that was supposed to extend the lead disappointed — **CagriSema missed (Dec 2024, Mar 2025), then failed its head-to-head vs Lilly's Zepbound (REDEFINE 4, Feb 2026)**; US pricing (MFN), compounding, and loss-of-exclusivity compounded it. The stock fell **~75% from the 2024 peak**; CEO Lars Fruergaard Jørgensen was ousted for **Mike Doustdar (Aug 2025)**; 2026 guided to *decline*. See [narrative map](../05_narratives/NOVO_NORDISK_narrative.md).

## 2. Perception evolution
1. **2016 — "insulin-pricing casualty":** US-pricing fear; deep de-rate.
2. **2021–2024 — "the GLP-1 once-in-a-generation winner":** priced for dominance, premium multiple, Europe's #1 by market cap.
3. **2025–2026 — "the fallen darling losing to Lilly":** priced for decline; washed-out; the bull hope = **amycretin (24% weight loss/36 wks, Phase 3 AMAZE)** + the oral pill.

## 3. The lesson in the arc
Novo shows how violently a narrative-driven pharma re-rates **both ways** on *expectations vs reality*: the market paid up for "CagriSema ≥25%" promises, then punished the miss brutally. The current setup is the mirror of 2021 — sentiment washed out, low bar, binary pipeline catalysts.

## 4. Analyst sentiment (Jun 2026)
- Deeply de-rated vs its own history and vs Lilly; market cap ~$203bn (from a peak far higher). The bull is deep-value + amycretin + the oral pill; the bear is structural #2 status — see [factual file §0/§8](../03_companies/NOVO_NORDISK.md).
- **The debate:** turnaround (US-share stabilises + amycretin hits) vs value trap (Lilly keeps winning). **Watch weekly US script share + the AMAZE readouts.** Largely macro-uncorrelated — a book diversifier.
