# Unilever — 10-Year History, Trajectory & Analyst Sentiment

> The decade pivoted on one event — the **2017 Kraft Heinz bid** — which turned a sustainability-led EM-growth darling into a margin-and-portfolio-focused (and often underperforming) staple now mid-reshaping. Companions: [factual](../03_companies/UNILEVER.md), [narrative](../05_narratives/UNILEVER_narrative.md). Research date 2026-06-28.

---

## 1. The arc & the leadership chain
- **Paul Polman (2009–2018):** the "sustainable, multi-stakeholder, EM-growth" era — delivered **~190% shareholder return** over his tenure ([HBS case](https://www.hbs.edu/faculty/Pages/item.aspx?num=52733)). Acquired Dollar Shave Club + Seventh Generation (2016).
- **★ Feb 2017 — the Kraft Heinz $143bn bid (rejected):** the defining event. KHC's margins (22.6%) dwarfed Unilever's (15.3% in 2016), exposing an efficiency gap. Unilever rejected it but then **accelerated margin, cash and portfolio actions** — sold the spreads business to KKR, raised margin targets, started buybacks. The "do more for shareholders" pressure has shaped it ever since ([BSIC](https://bsic.it/kraft-heinzs-ambitious-bid-unilever-fail/)).
- **Alan Jope (2019–2022):** struggled with volume growth + the failed £50bn bid for GSK Consumer Health (2022) which drew activist **Nelson Peltz/Trian** onto the board.
- **Hein Schumacher (2023–2025) → Fernando Fernandez (2025–):** the "Growth Action Plan" — focus on 30 Power Brands, productivity, and **active portfolio surgery** (tea/ekaterra sold 2022; **Ice Cream demerged Dec 2025**; **Foods → McCormick 2026**).

## 2. Perception evolution
1. **2009–2018 — "the sustainability + EM compounder":** premium-rated, Polman halo.
2. **2019–2023 — "the underperforming, volume-challenged staple":** weak volumes, EM FX drag, the GSK-bid misstep, activist pressure → a "show-me" stock that lagged Nestlé/peers.
3. **2024–2026 — "the reshaping turnaround":** volume growth returning (Q1-26 +2.9%), portfolio focus (Ice Cream out, Foods → McCormick), GLP-1-light RemainCo (see [narrative map](../05_narratives/UNILEVER_narrative.md)).

## 3. Key inflection points
- **2017:** Kraft Heinz bid → the efficiency wake-up.
- **2018:** spreads sold to KKR (~€6.8bn); the abandoned Anglo-Dutch dual-structure simplification (later unified to London, 2020).
- **2022:** failed GSK Consumer bid → Peltz/Trian activism.
- **Dec 2025:** Ice Cream (Magnum Co) demerger.
- **Mar 2026:** Foods → McCormick combination.

## 4. Analyst sentiment (Jun 2026)
- Premium ~23× multiple; ~3.4% yield; the bull is the reshaped, volume-growing, focused RemainCo, the bear is "EM can't offset saturated DM" (Barclays) + the premium price — see [factual file §0/§8](../03_companies/UNILEVER.md).
- **The debate:** does portfolio focus + returning volume justify the premium, or is it a perennially-"show-me" staple? **Watch the USG *volume* line — that's the whole quality case.**
