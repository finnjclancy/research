# ABB — 10-Year History, Trajectory & Analyst Sentiment

> From **sprawling, underperforming conglomerate** to **focused, high-margin electrification/automation winner** — a multi-CEO portfolio-and-operating transformation, now riding the data-centre/AI-power wave. Companions: [factual](../03_companies/ABB.md), [narrative](../05_narratives/ABB_narrative.md). Research date 2026-06-28.

---

## 1. The transformation arc (the key to ABB)
**2015–2019 — Spiesshofer & the activist pressure:** under CEO Ulrich Spiesshofer, ABB ran the **"Next Level"** strategy + the **White Collar Productivity program** ($1.3bn cost target). **Cevian Capital (activist) pushed to spin off the underperforming Power Grids division (2016)**; management resisted at first (2017) ([Utility Dive](https://www.utilitydive.com/news/in-major-restructuring-abb-shifts-from-heavy-electrical-manufacturing-to-d/544823/), [Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/investing/2019/08/30/abb.aspx)). Growth was sluggish (Q3-17: orders +5%, revenue +3%) — ABB was a chronic "value/turnaround" name.

**2018–2020 — the Power Grids exit:** ABB **sold Power Grids to Hitachi (~$11bn)** ([PowerMag](https://www.powermag.com/hitachi-acquires-abb-power-grids-business-in-11-billion-deal/)) — shedding heavy electrical T&D to pivot toward distributed energy + automation. A defining simplification.

**2019–2024 — the Rosengren re-rate:** Spiesshofer left (Apr 2019); **Björn Rosengren (ex-Sandvik) became CEO (Aug 2019)** and installed a **decentralised "ABB Way"** operating model — pushing P&L accountability down, divesting non-core (IPO'd Accelleron/turbocharging 2022, sold E-mobility stakes), and **structurally lifting margins**. This is when ABB re-rated from "underperformer" to "quality compounder." (Morten Wierod succeeded Rosengren as CEO in 2024.)

**2025–2026 — the AI-power pure-play:** record **$36.8bn orders, 19% margin, 25% ROCE**; **Robotics sold to SoftBank ($5.375bn)**; triple-digit data-centre order growth (see [narrative map](../05_narratives/ABB_narrative.md)).

## 2. Perception evolution
1. **2015–2019 — "sprawling underperformer":** conglomerate discount, activist target, sluggish growth.
2. **2019–2023 — "the Rosengren decentralization turnaround":** margin expansion + portfolio focus → re-rate.
3. **2024–2026 — "electrification + data-centre winner":** the AI-power equipment super-theme; now trades *above* analyst targets.

## 3. Key inflection points
- **2016:** Cevian activist campaign (spin Power Grids).
- **2018–2020:** Power Grids → Hitachi (~$11bn) — the big simplification.
- **Aug 2019:** Rosengren CEO — the operating-model overhaul.
- **2022:** Accelleron spin-off — portfolio focus.
- **2024:** Wierod CEO; data-centre orders inflect.
- **2025:** record orders + Robotics → SoftBank.

## 4. Analyst sentiment (Jun 2026)
- Premium quality multiple; **trading ABOVE the average analyst target (~CHF 81.6 vs ~CHF 73)** — the market has fully paid for the electrification/data-centre exposure + record execution; limited valuation cushion (see [factual file §0/§8](../03_companies/ABB.md)).
- **The debate:** is the data-centre/electrification order surge a multi-year structural super-cycle (justifying the premium), or a late-cycle peak exposed to a hyperscaler capex slowdown ([S3](../01_macro/MACRO_SCENARIO_TREE.md))? **Watch order momentum — especially Electrification.**
