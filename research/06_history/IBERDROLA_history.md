# Iberdrola — 10-Year History, Trajectory & Analyst Sentiment

> The opposite of Enel's drama: **two decades of consistent, compounding strategy under one leader (Ignacio Galán)** — international expansion into regulated networks + renewables, funded by record-and-rising capex. A steady premium compounder. Companions: [factual](../03_companies/IBERDROLA.md), [narrative](../05_narratives/IBERDROLA_narrative.md). Research date 2026-06-28.

**The shape:** net profit roughly **2.5×'d over the decade** (~€2.4bn → €6.3bn) on a steady, capex-funded compounding model — far less volatile than Enel.

## 2. The Galán strategy (the consistency is the story)
- **Ignacio Galán — Executive Chairman since 2006**; has invested **>€175bn in renewables, smart grids and storage** over two decades, building Iberdrola into **Europe's largest utility by market cap** ([Sustainability Mag](https://sustainabilitymag.com/news/ignacio-galan-iberdrola-ceo-clean-energy-giant)).
- **International diversification** is the throughline: ScottishPower (UK, 2007), Avangrid (US, growing since 2008→100% by 2024), Neoenergia (Brazil), Mexico — deliberately tilting to **regulated networks in strong-credit (A-rated) jurisdictions**.
- **Share price: ~€3.50 (2001) → >€20 (Jun 2026)**, plus **>€7/share of dividends** — and **10-year TSR that beat the top-3 US utilities, Euro STOXX Utilities, and the IBEX 35** ([Iberdrola](https://www.iberdrola.com/shareholders-investors)).

## 3. Perception evolution
1. **2015–2019 — "the steady networks+renewables compounder":** consistent capex-driven growth, US (Avangrid) accretion; a quality premium utility.
2. **2020–2022 — "the ESG/electrification leader":** the renewables/grid super-theme; premium multiple.
3. **2023–2024 — "rate-pressured but resilient":** higher yields pressured the bond-proxy multiple, but the networks-tilt + US/UK quality held up better than peers.
4. **2025–2026 — "the AI-electricity utility":** taking *equity* in AI data centres (Echelon JV), MSFT PPAs — re-rating story as an AI-infra play; €60bn 4-yr plan (see [narrative map](../05_narratives/IBERDROLA_narrative.md)).

## 4. Analyst sentiment (Jun 2026)
- A premium-rated quality utility (~16× fwd, ~10.8× EV/EBITDA); analyst PT (~€19.3) sits *below* the ~€21.75 spot — i.e. the market has *already paid up* for the quality + AI-power optionality; consensus sees limited near-term upside (Hold-ish) — see [factual file §0/§8](../03_companies/IBERDROLA.md).
- **The debate:** does the AI-power/data-centre + RAB-growth story justify the premium *and* the rate sensitivity, or is it priced for perfection in a higher-for-longer world? **vs Enel:** higher quality, more regulated/US-UK, steadier — but a richer multiple with less re-rating slack.
