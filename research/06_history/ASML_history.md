## 2. Stock performance & perception evolution
- **10-year total return ~+1,497% (~33.8% CAGR)** — $1,000 → ~$14,970 ([MacroTrends](https://www.macrotrends.net/stocks/charts/ASML/asml-holding/stock-price-history)). One of the great mega-cap compounders.
- **The perception re-rate (the real story):**
  1. **2015–2017 — "the EUV show-me":** ASML was a respected but *cyclical* litho supplier. EUV had been ~15 years and >€6bn of R&D in development and was still doubted. The multiple reflected skepticism. **Pre-COVID, the question was "will EUV ever work in volume?"**
  2. **2018–2019 — "EUV works":** 18 systems shipped in 2018; TSMC took 7nm to EUV. The monopoly thesis crystallised — *only* ASML can do EUV. The stock began re-rating from "cyclical" to "monopoly compounder."
  3. **2020–2022 — "the semis super-cycle":** COVID-era chip demand + the everything-shortage; ASML became a core secular-growth holding. Multiple expanded.
  4. **2023–2024 — "the cyclical reminder":** the Oct-2024 bookings miss (−16%, biggest since 1998) and the China-pull-forward wobble reminded everyone the bookings are lumpy — a perception check.
  5. **2025–2026 — "the AI toll-booth":** AI capex re-accelerated bookings (Q4-25 €13.2bn); ASML became *the* AI-infrastructure chokepoint trade. Re-rated to ~60× and all-time highs (~€1,711 in Jun-26). See [narrative map](../05_narratives/ASML_narrative.md).

## 3. Key inflection points (how it traded on news)
- **2012:** customer co-investment program (Intel/TSMC/Samsung take equity stakes to fund EUV) — validated the bet.
- **2018:** EUV volume shipments → the "monopoly" re-rate begins.
- **2022:** US/Dutch **export controls** on China begin — the geopolitical overhang is born.
- **15 Oct 2024:** bookings €2.6bn vs €5.4bn expected → **−16%** (the cyclical-reminder shock).
- **16 Jul 2025:** "cannot confirm 2026 growth" → **−11%** (~$30bn wiped).
- **Q4-25 (Jan-26):** €13.2bn bookings → the AI re-acceleration → run to all-time highs.

## 4. Current sell-side / buy-side sentiment (Jun 2026)
- **Consensus: Buy / Strong Buy** — recent tallies show **13 Buy, 0 Hold, 0 Sell** ([Public](https://public.com/stocks/asml/forecast-price-target)); near-unanimous bullishness.
- **Price targets** cluster roughly **$1,650–$2,345** (ADR), avg ~$2,019 over 3 months — i.e. upside to the ~all-time-high spot ([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/stocks/NASDAQ/ASML/forecast/), [WallStreetZen](https://www.wallstreetzen.com/stocks/us/nasdaq/asml/stock-forecast)).
- **Recent broker moves:** **JPMorgan → $2,200** (from $1,813), **BofA → $2,345**, **Bernstein $1,971** (Mar-26, ~35% upside). The bull thesis: dual-cycle (logic + HBM/DRAM), the >110-tool capacity-ceiling lift, and the €45bn backlog. **Buy-side caveat:** Morningstar has flagged the ~60× multiple as full (the lone bearish voice).
- **Net read:** the Street is crowded-long and bullish; the *risk* is that everything good is known — the asymmetry is to a bookings/AI-capex disappointment (the [S3](../01_macro/MACRO_SCENARIO_TREE.md) risk), not to more good news.
