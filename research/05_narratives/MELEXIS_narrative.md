# Melexis — Narrative Map

> Narrative-first companion to [`../03_companies/MELEXIS.md`](../03_companies/MELEXIS.md). Research date 2026-06-28. **The key framing: Melexis is a *semi* but NOT an AI stock — don't let AI narratives bleed into it. Its stories are the auto cycle, China localization, and an emerging robotics-content angle.**

---

## 1. The sector's narrative weather (automotive/industrial sensors)

**The dominant story:** *"Cyclical trough → H2-2026 recovery, on a structural content-per-car uptrend."* The market is waiting for the **auto-chip inventory correction to end**; analysts flag a **possible trough in H1 2026 before recovery as inventories normalize** ([Seeking Alpha](https://seekingalpha.com/article/4748399-melexis-stock-attractive-speculative-position-semiconductor-cyclical-recovery)). Melexis itself guides **flat H1, growth H2 2026.**

**Percolating sub-narratives:**
1. **★ "China Speed" — the localization pivot (newest, most important):** Melexis set up a **wholly-owned entity (WFOE) in Shanghai (Mar 2026)** and a **Chinese wafer-fab partner (production from 2026)** to build "**in China, for China**." APAC is already **~60% of sales, Greater China ~half of that**; China CAGR **~14%** (out-growing competitors) ([BriefGlance](https://briefglance.com/articles/melexis-bets-big-on-china-with-new-autonomous-china-speed-strategy), [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/news/melexis-establishes-wholly-owned-china-entity-to-deepen-localization-strategy-2032015428304355329)).
2. **"EV slowdown pressures content"** — the bear: European EV growth **slowed to single digits (from ~25% in 2025)** on incentive cuts, pressuring sensor content per vehicle ([ad-hoc-news](https://www.ad-hoc-news.de/boerse/news/ueberblick/melexis-nv-stock-faces-pressure-amid-semiconductor-inventory-adjustments/68977464)).
3. **★ "Robotics as the new TAM" (emerging optionality):** Melexis is explicitly targeting **industrial robotics + humanoid robots** as a sensor-content growth vector — a way to diversify beyond autos.
4. **"China local competition"** — the double edge of localization: going local wins share but invites domestic Chinese sensor rivals competing on price.

---

## 2. Where Melexis sits — a *content-growth* play hostage to *two cycles*

Melexis's narrative is a tug-of-war: **structural content growth** (more sensors per EV/ADAS/robot) vs **two cyclical/secular drags** (the auto inventory cycle + Europe's EV-demand wobble). The localization pivot is its attempt to convert the China-risk into a China-*growth* story. **It is the one name in the book where the "China" narrative is a net *positive growth* story (via localization), not just a risk** — the inverse of Kone/Carlsberg.

## 4. ★ Bull narratives that re-rate it UP

1. **★ "The trough is in"** — H2-2026 sales re-accelerate as guided; inventory correction ends. *Perception shift:* from "falling cyclical" to "early-cycle recovery + content compounder" → the cyclical re-rate (these moves are sharp off a trough).
2. **"China Speed delivers"** — localization wins share and China outgrowth (14% CAGR) shows up in design wins (e.g. NIO). *Catalyst:* China design-win announcements.
3. **"Robotics content materializes"** — humanoid/industrial-robot sensor wins give a *new* TAM narrative independent of autos.
4. **"Content-per-car re-accelerates"** — next-gen EV/ADAS platforms lift Melexis chips-per-car despite flattish unit volumes.

## 5. ★ Bear narratives that re-rate it DOWN

1. **★ "EV demand stalls"** — European/global EV growth keeps slowing on incentive removal → content-per-car growth fails to offset weak unit volumes.
2. **"China eats its own"** — Chinese domestic sensor makers undercut Melexis in its fastest-growth market; the localization bet backfires on margin.
3. **"Recovery slips"** — the H2-2026 inflection doesn't come; auto production weakens → another down year.
4. **"Margin compression"** — price competition + localization capex pressure the ~40% GM / ~17% OM that underpins the dividend.

## 6. The narrative-shift tells
- **★ Sequential (H1→H2) sales trajectory + lead-time/bookings commentary** — the trough-confirmation tell.
- **China design wins + "China Speed" execution** (the WFOE/fab ramp).
- **European EV registration data (ACEA)** + global auto production (SAAR).
- **Peer reads — NXP, Infineon, STMicro** auto-semi commentary (sector-wide inventory signal).
- **Any robotics content announcements** (the optionality re-rate).
- **Margin trend** vs the ~40%/17% guide.

## 7. Scenario read-through
- **Auto-cycle recovery (idiosyncratic):** the main upside — a clean early-cyclical re-rate.
- **S13 (Chip Cold War / China decoupling):** double-edged — localization protects China sales but a hard decouple raises local-competition + complexity.
- **Global recession (S3-adjacent):** autos are discretionary big-ticket — a downturn hits unit volumes and delays the recovery. ↓
- **Robotics boom (new trend):** call-option upside not yet in the price.
