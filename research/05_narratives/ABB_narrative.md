# ABB — Narrative Map

> Narrative-first companion to [`../03_companies/ABB.md`](../03_companies/ABB.md). Research date 2026-06-28.

---

## 1. The sector's narrative weather (electrical/automation industrials)

**The dominant story:** *"the picks-and-shovels of the AI-power build-out."* Industrials with electrical-equipment exposure have been re-rated as **the equipment layer of the AI/data-centre electricity boom** — the physical kit (switchgear, transformers, medium-voltage power distribution, UPS) that data centres and grids must buy. This is the **same AI-power megatrend** driving the utilities, expressed on the *equipment* side. Secondary themes: **reshoring/re-industrialisation**, grid modernisation, and electrification of transport/buildings.

---

## 2. Where ABB sits — a *pure-play* AI-power + reshoring winner

ABB is positioned almost perfectly for the dominant story:
- **★ Data-centre electrification is exploding in its order book:** Q1-26 **electrification orders +44% comparable**, with **triple-digit data-centre order growth**; ABB sees data-centre power capacity potentially reaching **>200 GW (~4× current)** ([Futurum](https://futurumgroup.com/insights/abb-q1-fy-2026-earnings-driven-by-data-center-and-grid-demand/), [Data Centre Magazine](https://datacentremagazine.com/news/abb-booming-with-record-us-orders-and-data-centre-growth)).
- **★ Reshoring/tariff-insulated:** ABB makes **~80% of what it sells in the US domestically** — a hedge against tariffs and a beneficiary of US re-industrialisation ([Seeking Alpha](https://seekingalpha.com/article/4831119-abb-exits-robotics-market-aims-bolster-industrial-automation)).
- **Portfolio focus:** **sold Robotics to SoftBank ($5.4bn)** to concentrate on the higher-return electrification/automation core (and robotics had slowed on auto-sector tariff uncertainty).

So ABB's narrative = **AI-power equipment demand + reshoring + a focused, high-margin (19%) portfolio.** The market has paid up (stock trades *above* its average analyst target).

## 4. ★ Bull narratives that re-rate it UP
1. **★ "AI-power equipment super-cycle"** — data-centre + grid orders keep compounding (book-to-bill >1) → ABB reframed as a **multi-year structural growth** name, not a cyclical → premium multiple sustained/extended. *The core bull.*
2. **"Reshoring beneficiary"** — US re-industrialisation + tariff walls drive domestic electrical-equipment demand into ABB's US-made base.
3. **"Margin + capital return"** — price-cost gap closes in H2-26, margins push past 19%, buyback/dividend continue.
4. **"Portfolio purity"** — post-Robotics, a cleaner electrification/automation story earns a re-rate.

## 5. ★ Bear narratives that re-rate it DOWN
1. **★ "Data-centre capex digestion" (S3)** — the hottest part of the order book (triple-digit data-centre growth) is the most exposed to a hyperscaler capex slowdown; a cool-down hits ABB's growth narrative hard. *The dominant downside given how much is priced in.*
2. **"Late-cycle rollover"** — a recession hits short-cycle orders/book-to-bill; industrials de-rate fast.
3. **"Priced for perfection"** — trading above target price; in-line orders disappoint.
4. **"Price-cost gap persists"** — if the electrification margin squeeze doesn't close in H2-26 as guided.

## 6. The narrative-shift tells
- **★ Order intake & book-to-bill, especially Electrification / data-centre** — the leading indicator; deceleration is the crack.
