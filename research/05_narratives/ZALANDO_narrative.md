# Zalando — Narrative Map

> Narrative-first companion to [`../03_companies/ZALANDO.md`](../03_companies/ZALANDO.md). Research date 2026-06-28.

---

## 1. The sector's narrative weather (European online fashion)

**The dominant story:** *"scaled platforms vs the Chinese ultra-fast-fashion invasion."* European online fashion is being reshaped by **Shein & Temu**, which now generate **~16% of online fashion purchase volumes** and are **shifting ad budgets *into Europe*** ([FashionNetwork](https://us.fashionnetwork.com/news/Retailers-lose-online-momentum-as-shein-temu-and-vinted-rise,1764312.html)). Two cross-currents define the sector: **(1) the AI-personalisation arms race** (predictive algorithms creating demand), and **(2) cross-border consolidation** — cross-border is set to be **45% of European fashion online by 2026 (~€105bn)**. The platforms that win are the scaled ones with logistics + data.

---

## 2. Where Zalando sits — the *scaled European champion* with a long share runway

- **★ Share runway is the bull frame:** Zalando holds only **~3.5% of the European apparel-and-footwear online market**, with management seeing **>15%** potential combining **B2C + B2B** ([Zalando business update](https://corporate.zalando.com/sites/default/files/media-download/zalando-se_business_update_2026.pdf)). It's **Germany's leading fashion player** (behind Amazon and Temu overall).
- **The ecosystem story:** B2C marketplace (62m customers, post-**ABOUT YOU** multi-app) **+ B2B (ZEOS logistics/software)** — a two-sided platform that monetises *other* retailers' logistics, not just its own sales.
- **The AI story:** AI-powered discovery/personalisation to lift engagement + efficiency — Zalando's answer to Shein/Temu's algorithmic hooks.
- **Positioning vs the invaders:** Zalando competes on **brand curation, quality, speed, and a premium/branded assortment** rather than ultra-cheap fast fashion — a differentiation that could be a moat *or* a vulnerability if consumers trade down.

## 4. ★ Bull narratives that re-rate it UP
1. **★ "Scale + ecosystem wins the consolidation"** — GMV growth (double-digit) *with* margin expansion, ABOUT YOU synergies (€100m, pulled to 2028), and B2B scaling → the **3.5% → 15% share runway** becomes credible. *Perception shift:* from "thin-margin e-tailer" to "European fashion *platform* compounder." *The core bull.*
2. **"Regulation clips Shein/Temu"** — tighter EU import/de-minimis rules erode the Chinese cost advantage → Zalando's relative position improves.
3. **"AI drives profitable growth"** — AI discovery lifts engagement *and* efficiency → margin expands toward mid-term targets.
4. **"Consumer recovery"** — a European real-income/sentiment upturn lifts discretionary apparel.

## 5. ★ Bear narratives that re-rate it DOWN
1. **★ "Shein/Temu compress the market"** — the Chinese platforms' European push forces promotions/margin down; Zalando's growth comes at the cost of profitability (cf. the Q1-26 cost-driven loss). *The dominant downside.*
2. **"Thin-margin discretionary in a weak consumer"** — a European downturn hits volumes at a **growth multiple** with **~4.8% margins** → punished hard.
3. **"AI arms race is a cost, not a moat"** — if personalisation is table stakes, it's margin-dilutive without differentiation.
4. **"Integration/execution"** — ABOUT YOU synergies or B2B scaling disappoint.
