## 1. The sector's narrative weather (semicap equipment)

**The dominant story:** *"ASML is the toll-booth on all AI compute."* Semicap has been re-cast from a boring cyclical into **the single chokepoint of the AI build-out** — no leading-edge AI chip (Nvidia, AMD, Broadcom ASIC, Google TPU, even Huawei via DUV) exists without ASML lithography. This is why ASML returned **~75% YTD** and trades at **~62× earnings** despite being a capital-goods company ([24/7 Wall St](https://247wallst.com/investing/2026/06/16/asml-could-soar-higher-in-12-months-and-heres-why-wed-buy-it/)).

**Percolating sub-narratives (ranked by how much they're moving perception):**
1. **★ The "dual-cycle" story (newly bullish):** logic capex *and* memory/HBM capex are firing together — when foundry logic peaks, EUV demand persists; when memory turns, SK Hynix/Micron lift DRAM-EUV orders for **HBM that feeds AI accelerators**. ASML rarely gets both at once ([24/7 Wall St](https://247wallst.com/investing/2026/06/16/asml-could-soar-higher-in-12-months-and-heres-why-wed-buy-it/)). This is the freshest leg of the bull narrative.
2. **The capacity-ceiling lift:** JPMorgan upgraded after concluding ASML can ship **>110 low-NA EUV tools/yr** (vs a prior ~90 ceiling) — i.e. the supply constraint that capped the bull case is loosening.
3. **The "AI-capex peak in 2027" debate** — the bear's clock: if hyperscaler capex digests (S3), EUV orders roll over and pricing power compresses.
4. **The China cliff + export-control wildcard** — China revenue guided down sharply in 2026; the swing between "trade-deal easing" and "tighter curbs."
5. **The valuation/"semicap bubble" sub-narrative** — Morningstar moved to **Sell on overvaluation**; the 62× multiple is the lightning rod.

## 2. Where ASML sits — it's the *neutral arms-dealer* of the chip war

ASML's unique narrative position: **it doesn't care who wins the chip war, only that the war is fought at the leading edge.** Every escalating front *increases* its addressable demand:
- **Nvidia vs the labs vs custom ASICs** (Broadcom/TPU/Trainium) → **more chip designers all racing to 2nm/sub-2nm** = more EUV/High-NA. (See [`AI_STACK_WARS.md`](../01_macro/AI_STACK_WARS.md) §3.)
- **Sovereign-AI build-out** → more fabs globally (TSMC Arizona, Intel, Rapidus Japan, EU Chips Act) = more tools.
- **China import-substitution** → SMIC still needs ASML **DUV** to scale Huawei Ascend — so even China's *anti-Nvidia* stack runs partly through ASML.

**The one thing ASML *does* care about: total leading-edge capex.** That's the bull's strength and the bear's only real weapon.

## 4. ★ Bull narratives that re-rate it UP (and the catalyst)

1. **"Dual-cycle confirmed"** — memory/HBM EUV orders accelerate alongside logic → a string of record bookings quarters. *Catalyst:* SK Hynix/Micron/Samsung HBM capex guides + ASML bookings beats. *Perception shift:* from "AI-capex proxy" to "structurally supply-short multi-end-market compounder" → multiple *expands* further.
2. **"The China cliff was the trough"** — China stabilises rather than falling off, removing an overhang. *Catalyst:* trade-deal easing / better-than-feared China guide.
3. **"High-NA inflects"** — adoption broadens beyond Intel (Samsung 1.4nm, TSMC A14P commit) → a new multi-year ASP/growth story. *Catalyst:* foundry High-NA orders.
4. **"Capacity unlocked"** — ASML lifts output >110 tools/yr and converts the €45bn+ backlog faster → upside to revenue estimates.

## 5. ★ Bear narratives that re-rate it DOWN (and the trigger)

1. **★ "AI capex is peaking" (the big one)** — a hyperscaler capex cut or an AWS/Azure deceleration breaks the demand story; ASML's lumpy bookings print a miss. *Perception shift:* from "toll-booth compounder" back to "high-beta cyclical at 62×" → multiple *compresses hard* (recall the documented −16%/−11% reactions). **This is the dominant downside.**
2. **"China export controls tighten"** — new EUV/DUV curbs cut the TAM and signal escalation. *Trigger:* US/Dutch policy headline.
3. **"Valuation air-pocket"** — even without a fundamental miss, a 62× semicap name de-rates in any AI-sentiment risk-off (circular-financing scare, Burry-style "bubble" flip).
4. **"High-NA disappoints"** — TSMC keeps skipping it, Intel's 14A slips → the next-leg growth story stalls.

## 6. The narrative-shift tells (watch these to front-run the re-rate)

- **★ Net bookings (esp. EUV) vs consensus** — the single most important number; the narrative lives and dies here.
- **Hyperscaler capex guides (Amazon/MSFT/Google/Meta)** + TSMC/Intel/Samsung/SK Hynix capex — the *leading* indicator for ASML orders 1–2 quarters out.
- **The CEO's forward-year confidence** (Fouquet) — a single "we can/can't confirm next year" sentence is worth ~10% (see factual file §7).
- **China % of sales + export-policy headlines.**
- **Whether the stock still *rises* on good AI news** — if beats stop being rewarded, the AI-capex narrative is tiring regardless of the print.

## 7. Scenario read-through
- **S4 (AI Vertical) / dual-cycle:** ASML is a top-decile winner — toll-booth + memory + sovereign fabs. ↑↑
- **S3 (AI Reckoning):** ASML is among the *most* exposed (high multiple + lumpy bookings) — overshoots down. ↓↓
- **S13 (Chip Cold War):** mixed — China TAM ↓ but DUV leverage + ex-China fab build-out partly offsets.
- **S1 (dovish pivot, oil down):** supportive for the multiple (long-duration growth re-rates up).
