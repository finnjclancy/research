**The headline:** Europe's largest online fashion platform, back in a confident growth phase — **GMV +14.7%, revenue +16.8%, 62m active customers**, the **ABOUT YOU acquisition** integrated (multi-app strategy), AI driving engagement/efficiency, and synergies pulled forward to 2028. The bet is on **platform scale + ecosystem (B2C + B2B/logistics) + margin expansion** against a still-soft European consumer and intense low-cost competition (Shein, Temu, Amazon).

## 1. The thesis & the narrative

**One line:** A scaled, two-sided European fashion ecosystem (consumer marketplace + B2B logistics/software) reaccelerating GMV growth while expanding margins, now enlarged by ABOUT YOU and leaning on AI for discovery and efficiency.

- **Bull story:** double-digit GMV/revenue growth resuming; **ABOUT YOU** integration adds scale + synergies (€100m, now expected a year early in 2028); the **B2B arm (ZEOS/logistics) more than doubled adj. EBIT**; AI-powered discovery lifts engagement; margin-expansion runway from 4.8% toward mid-term targets; buyback + cash generation.
- **Bear story:** **thin margins (4.8%)** and **discretionary/European-consumer cyclicality**; fierce competition from **Shein, Temu, Amazon and Inditex (Zara) online**; fashion e-commerce is promotional and returns-heavy; Q1-26 reportedly logged a loss on higher costs ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/zalando-logs-q1-loss-as-higher-costs-offset-strong-revenue-volume-growth-4661702)); integration/execution risk.
- **What the market pays for:** **GMV/active-customer growth + the trajectory of adj. EBIT margin** — growth *with* improving profitability is the re-rating combination.

---

## 2. The business — two engines

- **★ B2C (the core):** the consumer fashion platform — own retail + a marketplace (Partner Program) connecting brands to ~62m customers. Post-ABOUT YOU, a **multi-app approach** (Zalando + ABOUT YOU brands) widens reach. FY2025 B2C adj. EBIT €536m.
- **★ B2B (ZEOS / Zalando Logistics + software):** offering its logistics/fulfilment and software stack to other retailers — revenue €1.1bn (+14.6%), **adj. EBIT more than doubled** — a higher-margin, capital-leverage growth story ([Zalando FY2025](https://corporate.zalando.com/en/investor-relations/zalando-full-year-2025-results)).
- **★ ABOUT YOU:** acquired and integrated; the combined-group guidance and synergy plan (€100m by 2028, a year early) define the 2026–28 setup.
- **AI:** discovery feed/personalisation to drive engagement + operational efficiency — management frames 2026 acceleration partly on AI.

## 3. Income statement — the lines that matter

- **★ GMV (Gross Merchandise Volume):** €17.6bn (+14.7%) — the platform's top-of-funnel scale metric; **the #1 KPI** (captures marketplace + retail).
- **★ Revenue:** €12.3bn (+16.8%).
- **★ Adjusted EBIT & margin:** €591m, 4.8% group (5.3% Zalando standalone) — **margin expansion is the profitability thesis**; 2026 guide €660–740m.
- **★ Active customers (62m) & frequency/basket** — engagement/health of the consumer base.
- **B2B growth & margin** — the higher-margin diversifier.
- **Organic vs ABOUT-YOU-inflated growth** — separate the acquisition contribution from underlying.

## 7. ★ How the stock trades on news — the sensitivity map

> A **growth-discretionary platform**: trades on GMV growth and the margin path, with high sensitivity to consumer sentiment and competition.

**Ranked return drivers:**
1. **★ GMV & active-customer growth vs consensus** — the top KPI; acceleration/deceleration re-rates it.
2. **★ Adjusted EBIT margin vs guide** — profitability is the second leg; a margin miss (like a cost-driven quarterly loss) hurts even with good revenue.
3. **European consumer health** (real incomes, apparel demand, weather/seasonality).
4. **Competition** (Shein/Temu/Amazon/Inditex) — share and promotional intensity; any regulatory crackdown on ultra-low-cost imports helps.
5. **ABOUT YOU synergy delivery + B2B growth**; **AI-driven engagement** signals.
6. Rates/risk appetite (a long-duration growth name — sensitive to discount-rate moves).

**Reflex:** move ≈ (GMV growth surprise) + (adj. EBIT/margin vs guide) ± (consumer & competition). It's **higher-beta than the staples** — growth + thin margins amplify both ways; a margin disappointment at a growth multiple is punished.

## 9. Catalysts & narrative-shift radar

- **Quarterly results** (GMV, customers, adj. EBIT margin), **ABOUT YOU synergy milestones**, **European consumer data**, **competitive/regulatory news on low-cost imports**, **buyback**.
- **Bull confirmed if:** GMV growth holds in the double digits *and* adj. EBIT margin expands toward targets, ABOUT YOU synergies land early, B2B keeps scaling → re-rate to/through the PT.
- **Bear confirmed if:** consumer weakens, competition forces promotions/margin down (cost-driven losses), or synergy/integration stumbles.
- **The tell:** **GMV growth + the margin line together** — growth alone isn't enough at this multiple; the market needs *profitable* growth.
