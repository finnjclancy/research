**Pattern:** an **auto-cycle stock, NOT an AI stock** — read it off auto production (SAAR), the inventory cycle, and peers (NXP/Infineon/STMicro), never AI-capex. Early-cyclical: sharp moves off troughs (2019, 2023–25 down; 2021 up). Content-per-car is the structural uptrend under the noise; high-dividend, founder-controlled.

**Theses:** *Bull* — the trough is in (H2-2026 recovery) + "China Speed" localization wins share → cyclical re-rate. *Bear* — EV-demand stall + Chinese local competition compress margins. *Variant* — robotics (humanoid/industrial) sensing is a new TAM the market isn't pricing.
