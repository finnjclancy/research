## The big-move log

| Date | Move | What drove it |
|---|---|---|
| **15 Oct 2024** | **≈ −16%** (largest 1-day fall since 1998) | **Bookings shock + guide cut.** Q3 net bookings **€2.6bn vs ~€5.4bn expected**; 2025 sales guide cut to **€30–35bn**; CEO Fouquet cited "softer demand" outside AI + slow non-AI/China recovery; TSMC slowing 3nm adoption. *(Results leaked a day early via accidental publication, amplifying the shock.)* ([The Register](https://www.theregister.com/2024/10/21/asml_setbacks/), [Stocktwits](https://stocktwits.com/news-articles/markets/equity/asml-stock-plunges-after-leaked-earnings-reveal-forecast-cut-fuels-investor-concerns/cJcDd8ER4M)) |
| **27 Jan 2025** | **≈ −7%** (intraday more) | **The DeepSeek scare.** China's cheap-model breakthrough triggered an "AI capex is over-built" panic across the complex; ASML sold off on fear hyperscalers/foundries would cut spend. (Recovered as bookings stayed strong.) |
| **29 Jan 2025** | **≈ +viable bounce** | **Q4-24 bookings beat** (~€7.1bn vs ~€3.5bn fear) reassured the order book two days after DeepSeek — the print refuted the panic. |
| **16 Jul 2025** | **≈ −11%** (~$30bn wiped) | **Forward-guidance withdrawal.** Q2 *beat* (sales €7.7bn, bookings €5.5bn) **but** Fouquet said ASML "cannot confirm" 2026 growth, citing tariffs/macro. A single sentence on the *next year* overwhelmed a beat. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/07/16/asml-q2-2025-earnings-report.html)) |
| **Oct 2025 (Q3-25)** | **≈ +rally** | **Reassurance.** ASML said 2026 total sales would *not* fall below 2025 despite the China cliff — removed the Jul-25 overhang. |
| **28 Jan 2026 (Q4-25)** | **≈ +rally to ATH path** | **Record bookings €13.2bn (€7.4bn EUV)** → the AI re-acceleration confirmed; fed the run toward all-time highs (~€1,711, Jun-26). |
| **Mar 2020 (COVID)** | **≈ −30%+ peak-to-trough** | Broad COVID crash; recovered fast as semis proved resilient and the work-from-home chip demand surged. |

## What the big moves teach (the pattern)
1. **Bookings are the detonator.** The single biggest moves (Oct-24) come from **net bookings vs consensus**, not the reported quarter. A ~50% bookings miss = ~double-digit one-day move.
2. **Forward-year *confidence* > the print.** Jul-25 proved a *beat can sell off* if the CEO softens the next-year outlook. Watch the language on year n+1.
3. **ASML is the AI-capex sentiment proxy.** It moves on *others'* news — DeepSeek (Jan-25), TSMC/Intel/Samsung capex, hyperscaler capex — often before its own numbers.
4. **The dip-buy reflex marks the narrative stage.** While bad news bounces (it has), the AI-toll-booth belief is intact; the day a capex-doubt headline *sticks* is the early warning.

## Fresh theses
- **Bull — "dual-cycle + High-NA leg":** logic *and* HBM/DRAM EUV fire together while High-NA adoption broadens (Intel 14A → Samsung → TSMC A14P); bookings keep beating an already-raised bar → the €44–60bn 2030 path gets de-risked and the multiple holds near 60×. *Catalyst: 2+ quarters of >€10bn bookings.*
- **Bear — "AI-capex digestion":** the first hyperscaler capex guide-down (Amazon) breaks the demand story; ASML's lumpy bookings print a miss and the 60× multiple compresses to a cyclical ~30× → a 25–35% de-rate. *This is the highest-impact downside in the book.*
- **Variant — "China is a smaller drag than feared":** the 2026 China cliff is well-flagged; an *in-line* China + AI offset is enough to beat the bear case the market half-prices. The asymmetry is to China simply not collapsing.
