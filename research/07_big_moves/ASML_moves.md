**Pattern:** bookings vs consensus + forward-year confidence are the detonators (a beat sold off in Jul-25). ASML is the **AI-capex sentiment proxy** — it moved −7% on DeepSeek (not its own news) and +11% on a bookings beat two days later.

**Theses:** *Bull* — dual-cycle (logic+HBM) + High-NA leg keeps bookings beating → multiple holds near 60×. *Bear* — first hyperscaler capex guide-down (Amazon) breaks the demand story → 60×→~30× cyclical de-rate (the book's highest-impact downside). *Variant* — China declines less than feared; an in-line China + AI offset beats the half-priced bear.
