**Pattern:** a **markets + flows** stock — AUM, fees, and inflows all move with equities; a drawdown hits all three at once. Cost of risk barely matters; the **monthly net-inflow trend** is the tell. Italian (BTP) beta + low free float (~70% Doris/Fininvest) → can gap on flow data.

**Theses:** *Bull* — the structural Italian-savings compounder; inflows stay resilient (~€9bn) despite deposit competition → premium sustained. *Bear* — savers flee to deposits/BTPs (inflow cannibalisation) *and* a market drawdown hits AUM/fees/inflows together. *Variant* — scarcity/takeout-optionality premium in the Italian wealth-M&A wave.
