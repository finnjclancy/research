# Zalando — Big Moves: what drove the largest single-day re-rates

> A high-beta growth-discretionary name — the big moves are **COVID boom/bust, profit warnings, and M&A**. ## The big-move log

| Date | Move | What drove it |
|---|---|---|
| **2020 (COVID boom)** | **+47% revenue; stock to ~€105.90 (Jul 2021)** | Lockdowns forced shopping online → demand surge; market priced the online-penetration shift as permanent. Added to the DAX. |
| **24 Jun 2022** | **≈ −17% (intraday)** | **Profit warning.** Cut FY adj. EBIT guide from **€430–510m to €180–260m**; revenue growth guide slashed from 12–19% to 0–3% — cost-of-living + return-to-store reversed the online wave. ([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2022-06-24/zalando-plunges-after-cutting-guidance-on-worsening-economy), [Retail Gazette](https://www.retailgazette.co.uk/blog/2022/06/zalando-issues-profit-warning-as-cost-of-living-hits-customer-demand/)) |
| **Full-year 2022** | **≈ −70%** | The COVID-darling round-trip: serial guidance cuts, rising logistics costs, weak consumer confidence; market cap collapsed from ~$28bn to ~$6.2bn. |
| **2024–25 (ABOUT YOU)** | **moves on the acquisition** | The ABOUT YOU deal + a return to profitable growth drove the recovery off the lows. |
| **Quarterly prints** | **±10%+** | Thin margins + a growth multiple = big reactions to **GMV growth AND the adj. EBIT margin** together (a cost-driven loss quarter sells off even with revenue growth). |

## What the big moves teach (the pattern)
1. **The COVID round-trip is the template** — the market extrapolated a demand pull-forward as permanent (€105), then de-rated ~70% on normalisation. *The live read-across for any "structural shift" that may be a pull-forward (incl. AI).*
2. **Profit warnings are brutal at a growth multiple** — the Jun-22 −17% came from a *guidance* cut, not a print.
3. **Growth alone isn't enough** — at this multiple the market needs **profitable** growth (GMV + margin together).
4. **High-beta to the European consumer** + rates (long-duration growth).

## Fresh theses
- **Bull — "scaled platform + AI + regulation clips Shein/Temu":** GMV growth holds double-digit *with* margin expansion, ABOUT YOU synergies land, B2B (ZEOS) scales, and tighter EU import rules erode the Chinese ultra-fast-fashion cost edge → the 3.5%→15% share runway becomes credible.
- **Bear — "Shein/Temu compress the market":** the Chinese platforms' European push forces promotions/margin down; a thin-margin discretionary name in a weak consumer at a growth multiple is punished.
- **Variant — "the B2B/logistics optionality":** the market prices Zalando as a fashion e-tailer and under-credits ZEOS (logistics/software for other retailers) — a higher-margin, platform-economics leg.
