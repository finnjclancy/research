# Amazon — Big Moves: what drove the largest single-day re-rates

> Moves on **AWS growth, margins, and (now) capex/FCF**. Pairs with [factual §7](../03_companies/AMAZON.md), [narrative](../05_narratives/AMAZON_narrative.md), [history](../06_history/AMAZON_history.md). Research date 2026-06-29. *(Intraday %/dates approximate.)*

## The big-move log

| Date | Move | What drove it |
|---|---|---|
| **29 Apr 2022** | **≈ −14%** | **Q1-22: first quarterly loss since 2015** (incl. a Rivian markdown) + weak guidance as the post-COVID e-commerce demand normalised. The start of the 2022 de-rate. |
| **28 Oct 2022** | **≈ −7%** | **AWS growth decelerated** + soft Q4 guidance → the "AWS is slowing" scare; part of AMZN's ~−50% 2022. |
| **10 Nov 2022** | **≈ +12.2%** (largest up day in 3 yrs) | **Macro, not company** — a soft US CPI print sparked a market-wide relief rally. (Like AMD, AMZN's biggest up day was a macro day.) |
| **2 Feb 2024** | **≈ +7–8%** | **Q4-23 beat** — AWS re-acceleration + the advertising business scaling + the cost-out/margin story landing. The "three-engine" re-rate. |
| **5 Aug 2024** | **≈ −8.3%** (largest gap-down in 3 yrs) | **Soft Q2 guide (1 Aug) into a macro air-pocket** — the early-Aug global carry-unwind + weak US jobs amplified a guidance miss. |
| **May 2026 → late Jun 2026** | **≈ −18% from the ATH** | **Capex/FCF fear.** Despite an AWS-led beat, ~$200bn of AI capex with FCF crossing zero spooked the market — capex flipped from virtue to vice. |

## What the big moves teach (the pattern)
1. **AWS growth rate is the master variable** — decel (Oct-22) sells off; reaccel (Feb-24) rallies, regardless of retail.
2. **Margins/cost discipline** drive the *up* re-rates (Feb-24); the market rewards profitability after years of "growth at any cost."
3. **★ Capex is now a *liability* in the tape** — the May-26 drawdown happened *on a beat* because of the $200bn/FCF-zero story. The reaction depends on which line the narrative is fixated on.
4. **High-beta to macro** — biggest up day (Nov-22) was a CPI print; big down days cluster with macro air-pockets (Aug-24).

## Fresh theses
- **Bull — "AWS reaccel validates the capex":** the $364bn+ AWS backlog converts, Trainium lifts AWS margin, advertising compounds (~$70bn), retail margin expands → the capex is reframed as investment and the stock re-rates. *Catalyst: 2+ quarters of AWS growth holding ~28%+.*
- **Bear — "AI capex overbuild (2022 rerun)":** Amazon *already lived* a capex-overbuild bust (2022, −50%); if AWS growth wobbles, $200bn capex + FCF-zero makes it the **trigger for the whole AI-complex de-rate** (its capex guide is the cleanest [S3](../01_macro/MACRO_SCENARIO_TREE.md) signal).
- **Variant — "arms-dealer of inference":** in a token-deflation world (open/Chinese models), AWS is the *relative* winner — it monetises whatever model wins; the market under-credits this optionality vs the capex fear.
