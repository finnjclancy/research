# Embracer — Big Moves (verified)

> A special situation — big moves on financing/corporate-action shocks, not the games cycle. Pairs with [factual §7](../03_companies/EMBRACER.md), [history](../06_history/EMBRACER_history.md). Research date 2026-06-29.
>
> **Verification key:** ✅ date & move confirmed · ◑ event/date confirmed, % approximate · ⚠ unverified.

| Date | Move | ✓ | What drove it |
|---|---|---|---|
| **2020 – Jul 2021** | **≈ +30× from the 2016 IPO to peak** | ◑ | The debt/equity-funded acquisition supernova (27 deals in 12 months; Gearbox, Saber, Asmodee) → briefly Europe's most valuable games company (>$11bn). |
| **24 May 2023** | **≈ −40%+ in a day** | ✅ | **The Saudi/Savvy $2bn deal collapsed** — a ~$2bn, 6-year contracted-development partnership (PIF-backed Savvy Games) fell through; left >$2bn debt; guide cut (~$965m–1.3bn → ~$655–840m); mass layoffs/restructuring. ([Axios](https://www.axios.com/2023/05/24/embracer-group-deal-collapse), [Game Developer](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/business/report-embracer-s-doomed-2b-deal-was-with-saudi-backed-savvy-games)) |
| **2023–2024 (restructuring)** | continued de-rating | ◑ | Studio closures, 900+ layoffs, 15+ cancelled projects; the over-levered empire unwound. |
| **Feb 2025 / Dec 2025 (spin-offs)** | re-rating of the parts (mixed) | ◑ | **Asmodee** listed (Feb-25, re-rated up); **Coffee Stain** listed (Dec-25, *de-rated*) — spin-offs don't guarantee a re-rate. |
| **2026 (Fellowship)** | corporate-action driven | ◑ | Remaining business → Fellowship Entertainment (LOTR/Tomb Raider), Nasdaq Stockholm listing path. |

**Pattern:** single-financing dependence is the killer — the −40% day was one deal falling through, not an operating miss. It's a corporate-action stock (deal collapses + spin-offs), not a P/E. Spin-offs cut both ways (Asmodee up, Coffee Stain down).

**Theses:** *Bull* — sum-of-the-parts + IP catalysts (Tomb Raider series 2027, LOTR film Dec 2027, new LOTR RPG). *Bear* — spin-offs disappoint (Coffee Stain precedent); hit-driven misses; poor visibility. *Variant* — deep-value, macro-uncorrelated diversifier.
