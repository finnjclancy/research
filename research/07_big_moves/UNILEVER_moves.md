**Pattern:** M&A is the single-day driver — the market cheered a takeover *of* Unilever (+12%) and punished a bad deal *by* Unilever (−10%). Capital-allocation discipline is policed. Operationally it grinds on the USG volume line.

**Theses:** *Bull* — reshaped RemainCo (higher-margin, GLP-1-light, returning volume) justifies the ~23×. *Bear* — EM can't offset saturated DM; premium multiple on low-single-digit growth de-rates. *Variant* — break-up/activist optionality continues (sum-of-the-parts).
