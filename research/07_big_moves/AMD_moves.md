**Pattern:** expectations are the whole game — AMD's *down* days (Apr-24, Oct-24) were on *beats* that missed a high bar (~58× forward). Biggest *up* catalysts: deal/validation news (MI300, OpenAI) and the macro tape (Apr-25).

**Theses:** *Bull* — inference share gains (ROCm matures, MI400 on 2nm) + deal backlog converts. *Bear* — custom ASICs (Broadcom/TPU/Trainium) bypass merchant GPUs; one MI400 slip resets a priced-for-perfection multiple. *Variant* — a durable "second-source premium" even if AMD never out-designs Nvidia.
