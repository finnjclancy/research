**Theses:** *Bull* — deep value (~7×, ~6.9% yield, 13.5% RoTE) + a market/Amundi rebound closes the discount to the ~€20 PT. *Bear* — a risk-off move hits fees/insurance/CIB *and* widens the OAT spread (double sensitivity). *Variant* — the highest-yield way to own the bank-NII trade.
