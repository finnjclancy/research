**Pattern:** the biggest *down* days are **systemic contagion** (Mar-23, you fall on *others'* failures) and **French political/sovereign shocks** (Jun-24 — the OAT spread is BNP's unique lever; it falls *less* than pure-French peers). The structural re-rate is the rate cycle.

**Theses:** *Bull* — NII second wind + AXA-IM fee pivot re-rates P/B toward 1.0×. *Bear* — recession lifts cost of risk (NII up but provisions up more), worsened if private-credit stress bites. *Variant* — penalised for French domicile but least French-exposed of the trio; OAT-spread easing is asymmetric upside.
