**Pattern:** China construction is the whole multi-year arc (boom 2010s → bust 2020s). The services/maintenance annuity dampens earnings-day downside. **The 2nd derivative matters** — signs China is *less-bad*/bottoming can re-rate it even with absolute weakness.

**Theses:** *Bull* — China bottoming + stimulus-funded modernisation ("two new" + treasury bonds) offsets new-build; margin recovers into the 12.3–13.0% guide → re-rate to ~€62 PT. *Bear* — China property deteriorates further; stimulus underwhelms. *Variant* — the services-annuity quality is under-priced vs the cyclical China drag.
